Delicious Grilled Lamb Chops

After marinating these lamb chops, grill to medium-rare or medium and enjoy the best meal of your life!

Home cooking always wins!

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 10 mins

Additional: 8 hours

Total : 8 hrs 25 min

Servings: 10

Yield: 10 lamb chops

Here's what you need:

  • ¾ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 4 bay leaves, crumbled
  • 10 lamb chops
  1. Mix soy sauce, oil, garlic, thyme, ginger, pepper, and bay leaves together in a bowl.
  2. Place lamb chops in a large zip-top bag or resealable container. Cover with the marinade. Refrigerate for 8 to 24 hours.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate. Wipe off marinade from the lamb chops.
  4. Cook lamb chops, covered, on the preheated grill until browned, 3 to 4 minutes. Flip using grilling tongs; do not use a fork as it will let out all of the juices. Close the lid and continue to grill to medium doneness, about 5 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 130 degrees F (54 degrees C). Be careful not to overcook.
